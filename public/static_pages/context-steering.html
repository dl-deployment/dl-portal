Context Steering

Interactive Behavioral AI Guide

1. Interest Map

Defines directional desires. The AI calculates "how much" it wants to move in each direction based on targets.

2. Danger Map

Scans for obstructions. Angles blocked by obstacles are filled with danger scores.

3. Masking

The magic step. We subtract the Danger from the Interest. Only safe, desired directions remain.

4. Decision

The AI picks the direction with the highest remaining value as its final velocity.
